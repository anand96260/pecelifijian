Peceli Fijian Methodist Church of America

Peceli Fijian Methodist Church of America was founded in March, 2010. Started of with few families gathered together in the garage for their devotion. The name Peceli came from the scripture "Genesis 28:16-19 Then Jacob awoke from his sleep and said, "surely the Lord is in this place, and I did not know it. And he was afraid and said "how awesome is this place! this is none other than the house of God, and this is the gate of heaven." he called the name of that place Bethel."

Through weeks of prayer and fasting, we so eager to hear the gospel and since we in a small group without any ordained minster or layman, we have the faith to start of listening to the gospel through Skype sermon straight from the methodist ministers from Fiji. In this, we gained the knowledge to create a website and posted all live sermon from Skype. We so thankful and blessed to hear and share the gospel in our own mother tongue(fijian).In doing so, we want the rest of the fijian all over the globe to hear the gospel. We so touched by the good remarks of the viewers that help them spiritually and most of all thank our Heavenly Father for giving us the idea and knowledge. "Philipians 4:13 I can do all things through Christ who strengthen me."
